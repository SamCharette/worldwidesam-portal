Keeping the Signal Bright

Jun 25, 2026

Today is less about proving the Orbit Log exists and more about making it useful. A living portal should show what changed without turning every update into a parade float.

The signal is wider than one page polish pass, too. EEMS picked up more of the real modernization work, backups got attention, the RPG atlas kept getting easier to read, Marvel Runner moved along, and even One Bullet Dungeon stayed in the constellation.

  • EEMS is getting closer to the boring-good version of itself: direct, .NET-first, and easier for workplace staff to maintain.
  • The RPG atlas is turning from a pretty map into a tool that can explain what is on the shelf and what might actually run tonight.
  • The public orbit is becoming a porch light for the whole cluster: small posts, quick launches, and enough context that someone can tell what is new.

That is probably the thread I want to keep exploring here: how a personal site can be a working map of the things being built, not just a polished front door.
