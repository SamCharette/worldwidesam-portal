Opening the Orbit Log

Jun 24, 2026

The solar-system landing page has always been a map first: planets, moons, and quick launches into the little constellation of Worldwide Sam projects.

Now it has a logbook too. The front page can point at the latest post without getting in the way of the orbit view, and the blog can carry short daily notes about what changed, what shipped, or what is worth poking next.

Tiny update, useful surface. Very on brand.
